## üê≥ Alternativa: Despliegue con Docker

```dockerfile

    PUPPETEER_EXECUTABLE_PATH=/usr/bin/chromium-browser

COPY . .

# Exponer puerto

# Comando de inicio
CMD ["node", "server.js"]
```

Y `docker-compose.yml`:

```yaml
version: '3.8'

services:
  andes:
    build: .
    ports:
      - "3000:3000"
    environment:
      - NODE_ENV=production
    env_file:
      - .env
    volumes:
      - ./logs:/app/logs
    restart: unless-stopped
```
